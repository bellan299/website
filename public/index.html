Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about JP's Liquor? Here are some quick answers about our hours, location, and what we offer as your local liquor store in Lino Lakes, MN.

What are your store hours?
We're open Monday and Tuesday from 2:00 PM to 9:00 PM, and Wednesday through Saturday from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM. JP's Liquor is closed on Sundays.
Where are you located?
Our store is located at 6501 Ware Rd, Lino Lakes, MN 55014. If you're looking for a liquor store near Lino Lakes or Circle Pines, we're just a short drive away with convenient parking.
What do you carry?
JP's Liquor offers a curated selection of premium spirits, fine wines, and craft beers, including popular national brands and local Minnesota favorites.
Do I need to be 21 to shop there?
Yes. You must be at least 21 years of age to purchase alcohol at JP's Liquor, and a valid ID is required for all alcohol purchases.
Is JP's Liquor veteran-owned?
Yes. JP's Liquor is proud to be a veteran-owned business serving the Lino Lakes and Twin Cities community for over 10 years.
